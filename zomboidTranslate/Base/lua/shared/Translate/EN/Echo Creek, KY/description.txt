<LEFT> <SIZE:small>  Echo Creek the perfect location for those wanting to explore the western reaches of Knox Country by road. It's rural Kentucky at its finest. The people here farm, pray, and like to keep to themselves. <LINE> <LINE>
So why not venture west to find Ekron with its famous gnome factory, and bustling community college? Alternatively take a trip south to Irvington and its famous speedway. Then, to the north, you'll find Brandenburg with its Pondview Shopping Center and the permanently docked PS Delilah steamboat. No matter which way you go, it'll be the trip of a lifetime!
